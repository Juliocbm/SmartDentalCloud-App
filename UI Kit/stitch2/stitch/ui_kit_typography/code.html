Paragraph Layout

The Anatomy of Precision

Precision in dentistry requires a clear view. In our interface, we achieve this through the Editorial Paragraph pattern. By utilizing a 1.625 line-height (leading-relaxed), we ensure that dense clinical notes remain scannable during high-pressure procedures.

Unlike legacy systems that compress data into tight rows, DentaCore embraces negative space. A standard paragraph gutter is defined as 1.5rem (24px), providing the necessary separation to prevent visual fatigue during 10-hour surgical shifts.

  • check_circle Max line length of 65 characters for optimal readability.
  • check_circle Hanging punctuation to maintain structural integrity.
  • check_circle Variable weights to guide the eye toward critical diagnostic data.
